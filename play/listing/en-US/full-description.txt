Hisabak is a calm, private personal finance tracker for Android. It turns your bank transaction messages into a clean, organized view of your money — and everything stays on your device.

No accounts. No sign-up. No ads. No tracking. Hisabak has no internet access at all, so your financial data physically cannot leave your phone.

ADD TRANSACTIONS IN SECONDS
• Share a bank SMS into Hisabak, or select its text and tap Hisabak — it reads the amount and merchant for you.
• Or paste a message, or add a transaction by hand.
• Hisabak recognizes the merchant and keeps your categories tidy.

BUDGETS THAT ACTUALLY WARN YOU
• Set a monthly limit per category.
• Get a notification at 50%, 80%, and 100% — before you overshoot, not after.

SEE WHERE YOUR MONEY GOES
• A dashboard with your balance, income vs. expenses, and spending trends.
• Break spending down by category and by merchant.
• Clear charts designed to be read at a glance.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
• Create categories with colors and icons.
• Group merchants into the categories that make sense to you.
• Review and tidy everything from one place.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• All data is stored only on your device.
• No analytics, no advertising, no third-party data sharing.
• Automatic cloud backup is disabled so nothing is copied off your phone.

Hisabak is built for people who want to understand their spending without handing their financial life to the cloud. Simple, fast, and yours.

Amounts are shown in your currency with the dirham glyph; income and expenses are clearly distinguished.
