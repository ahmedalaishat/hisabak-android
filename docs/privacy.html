Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Hisabak is a personal finance tracker for Android. It is built to keep your financial data entirely on your device. We never collect, transmit, sell, or share your transactions, brands, categories, or budgets. The only data that ever leaves your device is anonymous crash diagnostics and anonymous usage analytics, described below — never your financial information.

The short version: Hisabak has no accounts and no advertising. Everything you record stays on your phone. To help us fix bugs and understand which features are used, the app sends anonymous crash reports and anonymous usage analytics (which screens you open and which actions you take, in aggregate) through Google Firebase — and nothing else. These never include your transactions or any personal or financial data.

Data we collect

None of your financial data. Hisabak does not collect or transmit your transactions, brands, categories, budgets, or any personal information — those stay on your device. The only exceptions are anonymous, technical crash diagnostics and anonymous usage analytics; see Crash reporting and Usage analytics below.

Crash reporting

To find and fix crashes, Hisabak uses Google Firebase Crashlytics. When the app crashes, it sends Google a diagnostic report containing:

  • the crash stack trace and the app version,
  • basic device information (model, operating-system version, language, and a randomly generated app-installation identifier).

These reports never include your transactions, brands, categories, budgets, SMS content, or any personal or financial information — only technical details about the crash. Google processes this data on our behalf as described in the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation. Crash reporting is active in the released app and is disabled in development builds.

Usage analytics

To understand which features people use and improve the app, Hisabak uses Google Firebase Analytics. It records anonymous, aggregate interaction events such as which screens you open and which actions you take (for example, that a transaction was added, or that onboarding finished).

These events never include your transactions, brands, categories, budgets, SMS content, note text, exact amounts, or any personal or financial information. Amounts, when relevant, are recorded only as coarse, non-identifying size ranges. Google processes this data on our behalf as described in the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation. Analytics is active in the released app and is disabled in development builds.

Where your data is stored

Your transactions, brands, categories, and budgets are stored only in a private database on your device. Automatic cloud backup is disabled, so this data is not copied off the device by the system. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Bank SMS

Some distributions of Hisabak (outside the Google Play version) can read incoming bank transaction SMS to automatically create a transaction. When this feature is present:

  • SMS messages are read and parsed locally on your device only, to extract the amount and merchant.
  • Message content is never transmitted off your device and is not shared with us or any third party.
  • The feature requires your explicit Android permission and can be left disabled.

The Google Play version of Hisabak does not access SMS at all. In that version you add transactions by sharing a bank SMS to the app, selecting its text, or pasting it — all handled on-device.

Permissions we use

  • Notifications — to show you transaction confirmations and budget alerts.
  • Internet — used only to send the anonymous crash reports described above.
  • Receive SMS (non-Play builds only) — to auto-capture bank transaction alerts locally, as described above.

Children

Hisabak is intended for adults and is not directed at children.
